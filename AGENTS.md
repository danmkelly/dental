# BrightSmile Dental — No-Show Reduction Programme

## What this is
Five-agent AI workflow for reducing patient no-shows at BrightSmile Dental (45 clinics, 10,000 appointments/month, 35% no-show rate). Part of PGDip Science in AI for Business coursework (S3 02 Customer Engagement and AI).

## Agent pipeline
Researcher (Alex) → Designer (Maya) → Maker (Sam) → Marketer (Ella) → Manager (James synthesises)

## The solution
Five-touch reminder sequence (T0 chairside commit, T1 booking confirm, T2 7-day prepare, T3 48-hour confirm, T4 2-hour nudge, T5 post-no-show re-book). Target: reduce no-shows from 35% to 17.5%. Predicted 45-55% reduction.
